Technical Blog Part 3

  • What a responsive site is, and why responsiveness is important?

    • Responsive site is an approach web-based design to make the page of the website suits to any size of the screen. It is important because every device have different screen size and every people nowadays not only seeing website through the laptop but also phone.

  • What mobile first design is? and why it's important?

    • It is an approach of design responsiveness for smaller screens. It is important as most people around the world have smart phone and access the website through their mobile phone. Therefore, it is important for web developer to understand mobile first design

  • What are frameworks are? and their pros and cons?

    • It is a package or pre-designed that made up by of a structure files and folders of standardized code e.g. HTML, CSS and Javscript as a basis to build the website. The pros are it makes people easier to build their own website, because once they know how these operate then it will be easier for every people to build the website. However, the cons is also it also takes time and people need to have big patient to learn this tool as they have to know the rules and limitation of these tools.
